Pelvic Floor?

Do you leak when you run, jump or sneeze? Do you need to go at the worst times? Do you let your pelvic floor keep you from achieving your fitness or weight loss goals, or do you manage to hide your difficulty while wishing you wouldn't have to?


Pelvic floor dysfunction imcompasses a range of symptoms including mild incontinence to more serious conditions such as severe pelvic pain and even pelvic organ prolapse. Many women believe pelvic floor dysfuntion is simply a reality of life that can not be avoided or improved. Because of this, they neglet to strengthen these muscles. Unfortunately, weak pelvic floor muscles usually progress to more serious conditions as women age. In reality, pelvic floor dysfunction is not something you have to live with! The pelvic floor is part of the body's core; the center of strength, movement and balance. Therefore, training the body properly starts with training the core. You don't need to live with dysfuntion any longer! Together we can win!

After college Kristen had the privelege of becoming a mother to 4 amazing children. Desiring to restore her pelvic floor so that she could remain active, Kristen sought out advice but found it difficult to receive practical help. After becoming an ACE certified personal trainer, Kristen specialized in core and pelvic floor function in hopes to bring others the answers to pelvic floor damage prevention and restoration. She desires for others to ultimately experience the same freedom she has re-gained. Kristen's training approach is foundational and functional. She knows how to begin where the client most needs help, and build upon this to ultimately train clients to their fullest potential. So, whether they are struggling to walk a mile, or training for a collegiate sport, Kristen helps her clients reach the next level. These days you may spot Kristen sprinting down the street or leaping in the air as she plays with her children. She is excited to help others find freedom as well!
